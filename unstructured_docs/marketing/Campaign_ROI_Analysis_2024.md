# Campaign ROI Analysis 2024
**WOM Communications - Marketing Effectiveness Report**

## Executive Summary

Full year 2024 marketing investment of **$18 million** generated **25,500 MQLs** at a blended cost per lead of **$706**, delivering an estimated **$210 million in pipeline** and a marketing ROI of **11.7x**. This report analyses campaign performance by type, channel, and segment to optimise 2025 investment allocation.
