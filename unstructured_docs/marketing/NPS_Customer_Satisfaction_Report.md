# Customer Satisfaction Report Q4 2024
**WOM Communications - NPS and Customer Experience Analysis**

## Executive Summary

Q4 2024 customer satisfaction remained strong with overall NPS of +45, up 3 points from Q3. Partner satisfaction reached +52, demonstrating WOM's commitment to the channel-first model. Key improvement areas include onboarding speed and contact centre features.
