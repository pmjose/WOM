# Fibra Market Consolidation Analysis 2025
**WOM Communications - Competitive Intelligence**

## Executive Summary

The Chile Fibra and business communications market is experiencing significant consolidation, with major M&A activity, financial pressures on key players, and shifting competitive dynamics. This analysis examines the implications for WOM and strategic recommendations for capitalising on market disruption.
