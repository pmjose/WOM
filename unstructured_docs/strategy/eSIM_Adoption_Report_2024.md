# Digital Transformation Report 2024
**WOM Communications - Technology Adoption Analysis**

## Executive Summary

This report analyses digital transformation trends affecting WOM's customer base and the adoption of cloud communications technologies. Key findings include accelerating Teams Phone adoption, strong partner portal engagement, and growing demand for AI-powered features.
