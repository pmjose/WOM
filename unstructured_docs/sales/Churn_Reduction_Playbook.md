# Customer Retention Playbook
**WOM Communications - Churn Reduction Guide**

## Executive Summary

This playbook provides strategies and tactics for reducing customer churn across WOM's customer base. With current churn at 0.9% monthly (10.8% annual), our target is to reduce this to 0.8% monthly (9.2% annual), protecting $8M in annual recurring revenue.

## Early Warning System

### Churn Risk Indicators

| Indicator | Weight | Trigger |
|-----------|--------|---------|
| Support tickets increase | 20% | 3x normal volume |
| NPS decline | 20% | Drop of 15+ points |
| Usage decrease | 15% | 30%+ reduction |
| Payment issues | 15% | 2+ late payments |
| Key contact left | 10% | Champion departure |
| Contract approaching | 10% | <90 days to renewal |
| Competitor mention | 10% | Discovery question |

### Risk Scoring Model

| Score | Risk Level | Action | Owner |
|-------|------------|--------|-------|
| 80-100 | Critical | Immediate outreach | CAM + Manager |
| 60-79 | High | Same-day contact | CAM |
| 40-59 | Medium | Weekly review | CAM |
| <40 | Low | Quarterly check | Standard |

## Retention Playbooks

### Competitive Threat Playbook

| Stage | Action | Resource |
|-------|--------|----------|
| Alert | Identify competitor | CI team |
| Assess | Understand driver | CAM interview |
| Counter | Value proposition | Battle card |
| Offer | Retention discount | Deal desk |
| Commit | Lock-in | Contract extension |
| Review | Root cause | Win/loss analysis |

| Customer Value | Max Discount | Term Required |
|----------------|--------------|---------------|

## Proactive Retention

### Account Health Reviews

| Customer Value | Review Frequency | Participants |
|----------------|------------------|--------------|
| Strategic ($100K+) | Monthly | Exec, CAM, Customer |
| Major ($30-100K) | Quarterly | CAM, Customer |
| Standard ($10-30K) | Semi-annual | CAM, Customer |
| SMB (<$10K) | Annual | Digital touch |

### Health Review Agenda

1. **Satisfaction Check**: NPS, feedback
2. **Usage Review**: Adoption, trends
3. **Value Delivered**: ROI, outcomes
4. **Roadmap Preview**: New features
5. **Growth Opportunities**: Expansion needs
6. **Support Review**: Ticket trends
7. **Action Planning**: Next steps
