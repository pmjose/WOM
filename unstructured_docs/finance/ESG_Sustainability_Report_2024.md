## TCFD Disclosure Summary

### Governance

The Board oversees climate-related risks through the ESG Committee, which meets quarterly. The CEO has executive responsibility for sustainability.

### Strategy

Climate change presents both risks (extreme weather, regulation) and opportunities (cloud migration demand, efficiency services).

### Risk Management

Climate risks are integrated into the enterprise risk framework. Key risks include physical (data centre resilience) and transition (carbon pricing).

### Metrics and Targets

- Net Zero by 2040
- 100% renewable electricity by 2025
- 50% carbon reduction by 2030 (vs 2020)
