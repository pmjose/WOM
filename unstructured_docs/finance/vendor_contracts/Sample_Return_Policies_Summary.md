## SLA Claim Process

### Standard Process
1. **Identify Issue**: Log incident in vendor portal
2. **Document Impact**: Record start/end time, affected services
3. **Submit Claim**: Use vendor's SLA claim process
4. **Track Resolution**: Monitor claim status
5. **Apply Credits**: Credits applied to next invoice

### Escalation Matrix
| Level | Timeframe | Owner |
|-------|-----------|-------|
| Level 1 | 0-4 hours | Service Desk |
| Level 2 | 4-8 hours | Operations Manager |
| Level 3 | 8-24 hours | Operations Director |
| Level 4 | 24+ hours | CTO |
