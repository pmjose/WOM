# ARPU Trends Analysis H1 2025
**WOM Communications - Revenue Per Seat Analysis**

## Executive Summary

This analysis examines Average Revenue Per User (ARPU) trends across WOM's cloud communications portfolio. With **1.8 million Cloud subscribers** (up 50% YoY), WOM maintains strong unit economics despite some customers opting for lower-cost solutions in the challenging macro-economic environment. The Group added **587,000 subscribers** since June 2024, with Germany contributing significantly following the Starface and Placetel acquisitions.

## Key Observations

### Chile Market
- Cloud seat additions slowed to 23,000 (H1 2025) vs 48,000 (H1 2024) due to macro pressures
- PhoneLine+ benefiting from "spend less" trend (+32% subscribers in half)
- PSTN switch-off driving fibre migration but at lower margins
- MS Teams voice enablement accelerating (+56k users vs +28k H1 2024)

### Germany Market
- Double-digit proforma revenue growth in both Starface and Placetel
- 29,000 organic seat additions on proforma basis
- Significantly higher gross margins (70%+) due to owned IP
- Now represents **20% of Group gross profit**
