# CFO Demo Script (10 Minutes)
## Snowflake Intelligence Platform - WOM Chile

---

## Overview

This demo is designed for CFO and Finance leadership at WOM Chile, focusing on Q4 2024 financial performance, segment profitability, unit economics, and cash generation metrics.

---

## Opening (1 minute)

**Presenter Says:**
"Today I'll demonstrate how our Snowflake Intelligence Platform provides instant access to financial insights - from our Q4 2024 results through to segment profitability, regional performance, and subscriber economics - all through natural language queries."

---

## Demo Questions

### Question 1: Q4 2024 Financial Performance
> "What was our Q4 2024 financial performance? Show me MRR, revenue by segment, and gross margin."

**Expected Insights:**
- Monthly Recurring Revenue: $ 28.5M (+42% YoY)
- Total Revenue (Q4): $ 95.2M (+38% YoY)
- Gross Profit: $ 64.7M (+45% YoY)
- Gross Margin: 68% (+3pp YoY)
- **By Segment:**
  - Residential: $ 76.2M revenue (80% of total)
  - Enterprise: $ 19.0M revenue (20% of total)
- EBITDA: $ 28.6M (30% margin)
- Net Income: $ 14.2M

**Talking Point:** "Complete P&L visibility by segment in one question - enterprise growth accelerating."

---

### Question 2: Subscriber Unit Economics
> "What are our subscriber economics? Show me ARPU, CAC, LTV, and churn by segment."

**Expected Insights:**
- **Residential Segment:**
  - ARPU: $ 85/month (+9% YoY)
  - CAC: $ 180
  - LTV: $ 2,040 (24-month avg tenure)
  - LTV:CAC: 11.3x
  - Monthly Churn: 1.8%
- **Enterprise Segment:**
  - ARPU: $ 450/month (+7% YoY)
  - CAC: $ 850
  - LTV: $ 16,200 (36-month avg tenure)
  - LTV:CAC: 19.1x
  - Monthly Churn: 0.8%

**Talking Point:** "Strong unit economics across both segments - enterprise showing exceptional stickiness."

---

### Question 3: Regional Profitability
> "How does profitability vary across Chile regions? Which regions have the best margins?"

**Expected Insights:**
- **Santiago Metropolitana:** $ 62M revenue, 70% gross margin (scale benefits)
- **Valparaiso:** $ 12M revenue, 65% gross margin (fastest growing)
- **Maule:** $ 8M revenue, 64% gross margin
- **Concepcion:** $ 4.5M revenue, 62% gross margin
- **Other Regions:** $ 8.7M revenue, 58% gross margin (higher installation costs)
- Regional expansion investment: $ 8M CapEx for coverage

**Talking Point:** "Santiago drives profitability while regional markets show strong potential."

---

### Question 4: Cash Flow & Investment
> "What is our cash position, CapEx spending, and operating cash flow?"

**Expected Insights:**
- Operating Cash Flow (Q4): $ 22.4M
- Cash Conversion: 78%
- **CapEx Deployment:**
  - Network Infrastructure: $ 12M
  - Customer Premises Equipment: $ 4M
  - IT Systems: $ 2M
  - Total Q4 CapEx: $ 18M
- Free Cash Flow: $ 4.4M
- Cash Position: $ 35M
- Debt: $ 45M (bank facility)
- Net Debt/EBITDA: 0.4x

**Talking Point:** "Strong cash generation funding network expansion - low leverage provides flexibility."

---

### Question 5: 2025 Financial Outlook
> "What is our financial guidance for 2025 and key investment priorities?"

**Expected Insights:**
- **2025 Targets:**
  - MRR Target: $ 42M (+47% growth)
  - Revenue Target: $ 145M
  - Gross Margin Target: 70%
  - EBITDA Margin Target: 32%
- **Investment Plan:**
  - Network CapEx: $ 45M (regional expansion)
  - Marketing: $ 18M (customer acquisition)
  - Technology: $ 8M (systems upgrade)
- **Key Initiatives:**
  - Enter 3 new departments
  - Enterprise segment to 25% of revenue
  - Launch Business VoIP product

**Talking Point:** "Clear path to profitability improvement while investing in growth."
