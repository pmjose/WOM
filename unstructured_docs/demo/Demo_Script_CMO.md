# CMO Demo Script (10 Minutes)
## Snowflake Intelligence Platform - WOM Chile

---

## Overview

This demo is designed for CMO/Marketing leadership audiences at WOM Chile, focusing on campaign performance, customer acquisition, competitive positioning, and marketing ROI.

---

## Opening (1 minute)

**Presenter Says:**
"Today I'll show you how our Snowflake Intelligence Platform provides instant marketing intelligence - from campaign ROI to competitive analysis to customer acquisition metrics. Let's see how data-driven marketing is fueling WOM's growth across Chile."

---

## Demo Questions

### Question 1: Campaign Performance Overview
> "What were our top performing marketing campaigns in Q4 2024 and their ROI?"

**Expected Insights:**
- Lanzamiento Plan 5000 Mbps: $ 850K spend, 2,100 leads, 18.5x ROI
- Verano Sin Instalacion: $ 620K spend, 1,450 leads, 15.3x ROI
- Cambiate a WOM: $ 380K spend, 1,200 leads, 21.1x ROI
- Total Q4: $ 4.2M spend, 6,850 leads, 12.4x ROI

**Talking Point:** "Instant visibility into which campaigns drive the best results across all channels."

---

### Question 2: Channel Performance
> "Which marketing channels have the best cost per lead and conversion rates?"

**Expected Insights:**
- Facebook Ads: $ 45 CPL, 4.2% conversion
- Google Ads: $ 62 CPL, 3.8% conversion  
- Instagram: $ 38 CPL, 5.1% conversion (best efficiency)
- TikTok: $ 52 CPL, 6.2% conversion (highest conversion)
- TV Nacional: $ 85 CPL, 2.1% conversion (brand awareness)
- Referidos: $ 25 CPL, 12.5% conversion (best quality)

**Talking Point:** "Real-time channel optimization ensures we're investing in what works best for Chile market."

---

### Question 3: Regional Marketing Performance
> "How are our marketing campaigns performing across different Chile regions?"

**Expected Insights:**
- Santiago Metropolitana: 65% of leads, $ 48 avg CPL
- Valparaiso: 12% of leads, $ 55 avg CPL, fastest growing
- La Libertad: 8% of leads, $ 52 avg CPL
- Concepcion: 5% of leads, $ 62 avg CPL, tourism segment
- Other regions: 10% of leads, $ 58 avg CPL

**Talking Point:** "Regional insights help us tailor campaigns and allocate budget where growth potential is highest."

---

### Question 4: Competitive Analysis
> "How does our brand awareness compare to Claro and Movistar? What is our share of voice?"

**Expected Insights:**
- WOM brand awareness: 45% in Santiago (up from 32% YoY)
- Share of voice (digital): 22% vs Claro 35%, Movistar 30%
- Customer preference (fiber): WOM 38%, Claro 28%, Movistar 25%
- NPS advantage: +62 vs industry +35
- Speed perception: WOM leads in "fastest internet" category

**Talking Point:** "We're winning the perception battle in premium fiber - customers know WOM means speed."

---

### Question 5: Customer Acquisition Metrics
> "What is our customer acquisition cost and how does it compare to customer lifetime value?"

**Expected Insights:**
- Residential CAC: $ 180 (down 15% YoY)
- Enterprise CAC: $ 850 (stable)
- Residential LTV: $ 2,040 (24-month avg tenure)
- Enterprise LTV: $ 16,200 (36-month avg tenure)
- LTV:CAC Residential: 11.3x
- LTV:CAC Enterprise: 19.1x

**Talking Point:** "Strong unit economics prove our marketing investment is driving profitable growth."
