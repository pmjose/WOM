# CEO Demo Script (10 Minutes)
## Snowflake Intelligence Platform - WOM Chile

---

## Overview

This demo is designed for CEO/Board-level audiences at WOM Chile, focusing on strategic insights, market position, subscriber growth, and business performance based on the latest Q4 2024 results and Chile market positioning.

---

## Opening (1 minute)

**Presenter Says:**
"Today I'll show you how our Snowflake Intelligence Platform enables instant access to strategic insights across all of WOM's business operations - combining operational data with strategy documents in natural language. This is particularly timely as WOM continues to expand fiber coverage across Chile's major departments."

---

## Demo Questions

### Question 1: Business Health Overview
> "Give me an executive summary of WOM's Q4 2024 performance - subscriber count, MRR, ARPU, and our key growth metrics."

**Expected Insights:**
- 450K total subscribers, up 35% YoY
- $ 28.5M Monthly Recurring Revenue, +42% growth
- Residential ARPU: $ 85, Enterprise ARPU: $ 450
- 68% gross margin, up 3pp
- 92% recurring revenue
- Santiago Metropolitana: 65% of subscriber base

**Talking Point:** "One question gives you the complete health of the business including our expansion into regional markets."

---

### Question 2: Regional Expansion Performance
> "How is our regional expansion performing beyond Santiago? Which departments are showing the strongest growth?"

**Expected Insights:**
- Valparaiso subscribers: 42K (up 85% YoY) - fastest growing region
- La Libertad (Copiapo): 28K subscribers, strong enterprise uptake
- Concepcion: 15K subscribers, tourism sector driving demand
- Piura & Lambayeque: Combined 25K, coastal corridor growth
- Regional markets now represent 35% of new subscriber additions
- Average installation time in regions: 4.2 days (vs 2.8 days in Santiago)

**Talking Point:** "Our strategic investment in regional expansion is delivering exceptional results - we're becoming Chile's national fiber provider, not just a Santiago company."

---

### Question 3: Market Position
> "How is WOM positioned against Claro, Movistar, and other competitors? What is our market share trend?"

**Expected Insights:**
- WOM fiber market share: ~8% nationally, 15% in premium segment
- Claro: Largest overall, but hybrid network limits speeds
- Movistar: Legacy infrastructure, losing premium customers
- Entel/Bitel: Focused on mobile, limited fiber presence
- Win Chile: Similar positioning, regional competitor
- Customer NPS: +62 vs industry average +35
- Churn rate: 1.8% monthly (vs industry 2.5%)

**Talking Point:** "Real-time competitive intelligence shows we're winning the premium fiber market as customers demand true fiber speeds."

---

### Question 4: Product Performance & Upsell
> "Which internet plans are most popular and what is our TV Digital attachment rate?"

**Expected Insights:**
- Plan 1500 Mbps: Most popular (45% of residential)
- Plan 5000 Mbps: Fastest growing (+120% YoY), gaming/streaming segment
- TV Digital attachment: 42% of internet subscribers
- L1MAX Sports: 65% take-rate among TV subscribers
- WiFi 6 equipment: 78% of new installations
- Duo bundles (Internet + TV): 38% of new sales

**Talking Point:** "Our high-speed plans and content partnerships are driving ARPU growth and customer stickiness."

---

### Question 5: Strategic Outlook
> "What are the key metrics for the board and what is our 2025 outlook?"

**Expected Insights:**
- 2025 Subscriber Target: 650K (44% growth)
- 2025 MRR Target: $ 42M
- New regions: Junin (Huancayo), Ica expansion
- Enterprise segment target: 25% of revenue (from 20%)
- CapEx plan: $ 45M for network expansion
- New products: Business VoIP, Managed WiFi for enterprises

**Talking Point:** "Board-ready insights in seconds - we're positioned to be Chile's leading fiber ISP by 2026."

---

## Closing (1 minute)

**Presenter Says:**
"In 10 minutes, you've seen how this platform:
- Provides instant strategic insights from our Q4 2024 results
- Tracks our regional expansion in real-time
- Enables competitive analysis across Chile's telecom market
- Supports board-level decision making

As Chile continues its digital transformation, WOM is positioned to be the preferred fiber provider for homes and businesses. This platform brings that insight to your fingertips."
